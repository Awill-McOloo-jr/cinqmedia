How It Works

A production path that keeps decisions visible.

Three stages. Clear ownership at each one. The brief is agreed before production starts. The package is reviewed before it is published.

01

Brief

The brief defines the story, audience, evidence chain, format, timeline, and what done looks like.

02

Produce

The team reports, records, photographs, writes, edits, and fact-checks, with field and desk work running in parallel.

03

Package

Final files arrive platform-ready with metadata, caption sets, source documentation, and a useful handover note.
