Story Brief

25 Jun 2025, 06:00 Nairobi / National 12 min read

On June 25, 2024, a generation Kenya's political class had written off as apolitical walked through the gates of parliament and changed the terms of what civic participation could look like.

Forty-one people were shot dead by police. The Finance Bill 2024 was withdrawn within 48 hours. President Ruto fired his cabinet two weeks later.

Tutawaona, they said. We are still watching.
