See how the SHA investigation became a six-format media package.

One story brief. One FOIA request. Six weeks of reporting. This is how Cinq built the SHA package from the first source call to the final export file.

The Brief

Tell a Kenyan health story the way it deserves to be told.

The package began with one question: why were registered patients still being turned away at hospital gates? The work moved from patient interviews to document sourcing, service explainers, portrait photography, and short-format video.

  • Profile patients who paid contributions and were still denied care.
  • Map rejection hotspots and explain the policy in plain Kiswahili.
  • Deliver a long-form article, short videos, audio clips, quote cards, and source archive.

Deliverables
